PaperVault was born in a classroom to solve the stress of exam season.

Our Story

We noticed that students at Abdul Wali Khan University (AWKUM) were wasting hours scrolling through disorganized WhatsApp groups just to find study materials. PaperVault changes that.

We are a community-powered platform where students can seamlessly upload, organize, and access past exam papers. Whether it's a mid-term, a final, or a tricky quiz, we've got it organized.
