About PaperVault

Built for AWKUM Students

A community-driven repository of past exam papers — free, searchable, and organized so you can focus on studying, not hunting.

Our Story

Why We Built This

Students at Abdul Wali Khan University were spending hours digging through scattered WhatsApp groups just to find one past paper. PaperVault changes that — one organized, always-free platform where every paper is tagged, verified, and a click away.

Our Vision

Where We're Going

We started at AWKUM, but our goal is bigger — a centralized vault connecting students from every university across Pakistan.

"No student should fail because they couldn't find the right resource."
