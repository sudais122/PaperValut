About PaperVault

Your University's Complete Paper Archive

PaperVault is a free, community-powered platform where students upload and access past exam papers from their universities. Whether it's mid-term or final-term, we've got it organized.

Our platform is specifically designed for Pakistani university students who need access to course-specific, teacher-specific, and term-specific past papers — all in one easy-to-search place.

  • Filter by teacher, subject, course code, and exam type
  • Upload papers and help your fellow students
  • Access papers from multiple universities across Pakistan
  • Completely free — no hidden charges
