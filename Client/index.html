About PaperVault

Our Story

Why We Built This

Students at Abdul Wali Khan University were spending hours digging through scattered WhatsApp groups just to find one past paper before exams.

PaperVault changes that — one organized, always-free platform where every paper is tagged, verified, and a click away.
